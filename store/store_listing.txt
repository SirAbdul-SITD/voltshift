VoltShift: Power Grid Puzzle

SHORT DESCRIPTION (max 80 chars):

FULL DESCRIPTION:
🟢 SHIFT. ALIGN. POWER UP. 🟢

VoltShift drops you in front of a glowing retro terminal. The power socket is on the left. The lamp is on the right. Between them: a grid of conduit tiles that only YOU can move — by shifting entire rows and columns.

⚡ HOW TO PLAY
• Swipe a row left or right — every tile in it slides over
• Swipe a column up or down — same deal
• Tiles wrap around: push one off the edge, it reappears on the other side
• Align the conduits so power flows from socket to lamp

✨ FEATURES
• 150 levels: Easy 4×4, Medium 5×5, Hard 6×6
• One-of-a-kind wrap-around shifting mechanic
• Glorious green phosphor CRT terminal aesthetic with scanlines
• Live power tracing — watch the current surge as you shift
• Par system: solve at or under par for 3 stars
• Deep, hypnotic ambient soundtrack
• Haptic feedback
• Fully offline, ad-free, purchase-free, zero data collection

🧠 SIMPLE RULES, DEVIOUS DEPTH
Every swipe moves a whole line — fix one tile and you may break three. Think like a machine: the shortest solutions are beautiful, and the terminal is always watching your par count.
